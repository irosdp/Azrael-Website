About

Azrael Morathane

A modern gothic anime metal artist identity: ornate but sharp, romantic but heavy, built for midnight headphones and stage-sized impact.

Azrael Morathane exists between cathedral shadow and neon afterimage. The sound blends melodic metal, gothic atmosphere, electronic pressure, and anime-scale vocal drama without leaning into nostalgia.

The project language is black, deep burgundy, and pale pink: elegant, severe, and emotionally direct. Each release is treated as both a song and a visual entry in the same dark romantic world.
